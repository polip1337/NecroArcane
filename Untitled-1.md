### 40-Step Progression for Necromancer Elara

**Starting Resources (Step 1):** Family Home (safe base), Loving Family (bonds for support), Village Community (reputation for aid).

#### Steps 1-5: Act 1 - The Blighted Hearth
**Step 1: Childhood Awakening**  
**Gate:** None (tutorial start).  
Tend to daily chores with family and Shadow. Strengthen family bonds. Unlock  Herb Gathering action.

**Step 2: Herb Foraging**  
**Gate:** Herbs.  
Forage meadows with brothers. Harvest healing herbs. Boost family morale and village ties. Unlock Assist mother treating villagers action, uses herbs, generates knowledge.

**Gate:** Herbs, Knowledge.  
Start healing on your own. Earn gold and reputation from grateful patients. Unlock simple healing rituals. lock "Assist mother treating villagers"

**Step 4: Evening Prayers**  
**Gate:** Herbs, Knowledge.  
Offer gold to gods' shrine with family. Increases faith and reputation.

**Step 5: Aspirations Stir**  
**Gate:** Knowledge, faith, Gold.  
Study healing scrolls in the temple, action with finite uses, costs gold has a reputation/faith gate. Increases knowledge.

#### Steps 6-10: The Plague Descends
**Step 6: Ominous Whispers**  
**Gate:**  Reputation.  
Hear plague rumors; plead at shrines. Build initial grudge against silent gods. Faith wanes as fear rises.

**Step 7: Plague's Shadow Falls**  
**Gate:** Herbs, Faith, Family Bonds.  
Treat stricken brothers. Exhaust remedies; family weakens. Grudge intensifies.

**Step 8: Gods' Silence**  
**Gate:** Remaining Faith, Family Home.  
Vigil-prayers fail; brothers perish. Gain family corpses. Faith converts to grief mana. Village panics.

**Step 9: Final Losses**  
**Gate:** Family Home (barricade).  
Parents and Shadow succumb. Accumulate more corpses. Grief mana surges; isolation sets in.

**Step 10: Forbidden Discovery**  
**Gate:** Grief Mana, Family Corpses.  
Unearth Necro Tome; ritual on corpses. Humans fail to rise; Shadow becomes Undead Cat familiar. Swear vengeance.  
**Core Resources Unlocked:** Undead Minions, Necro Tome, Mana (regen), Souls, Corpses, Village Hideout (base).

#### Steps 11-40: Path of Vengeance
**Step 11: Mourn and Scavenge**  
**Gate:** Undead Cat, Mana reserves.  
Scout ruins for corpses and souls. Unlock Soul Harvest ritual.

**Step 12: First Minion**  
**Gate:** Corpses, Souls, Mana, Necro Tome.  
Raise basic Skeleton Warrior. Expand minion roster.

**Step 13: Plague Pit Raid**  
**Gate:** Skeleton Minion, Mana for stealth.  
Infiltrate pits for corpse haul. Evade guards.

**Step 14: Hideout Fortification**  
**Gate:** Corpses, Scavenged Gold.  
Build bone traps. Boost mana regeneration. Unlock base defenses.

**Step 15: Animal Allies**  
**Gate:** Souls, Mana.  
Raise undead rats for swarms. Diversify scouts.

**Step 16: Soul Siphon Practice**  
**Gate:** Mana reserves.  
Harvest from wildlife. Expand mana capacity.

**Step 17: Survivor Ambush**  
**Gate:** Skeleton Minion (as bait).  
Lure and harvest lone scavenger. Replenish losses; gain gold.

**Step 18: Ghoul Creation**  
**Gate:** Multiple Corpses, Souls, Mana.  
Evolve fleshier brute minion.

**Step 19: Tome Deep Study**  
**Gate:** Mana over time, Hideout.  
Unlock Minion Link (shared vision). Gain Knowledge.

**Step 20: Village Clearance**  
**Gate:** Growing Minion Force, Group Ritual Mana.  
Eliminate holdouts. Claim village territory. Recruit initial cultist followers.

**Step 21: Cult Foundations**  
**Gate:** Followers, Souls.  
Convert via risen dead. Build Corpse Pit for passive generation.

**Step 22: Road Tolls**  
**Gate:** Mana for illusions, Brute Minions.  
Ambush travelers. Accumulate gold and corpses. Unlock Fear Aura.

**Step 23: Zombie Beginnings**  
**Gate:** Corpse Stockpile, Souls, Mass Mana.  
Raise zombie horde. Build Bone Forge for skeleton production.

**Step 24: Rival Elimination**  
**Gate:** Ghoul Assassin.  
Defeat competing robber. Claim rival tome. Unlock Wight ritual.

**Step 25: Hamlet Conquest**  
**Gate:** Horde Army, Battle Ritual Mana.  
Overrun nearby settlement. Expand territory; recruit more followers.

**Step 26: Wight Summon**  
**Gate:** Rival Corpse, Souls, Mana.  
Raise commanding lieutenant. Boost army efficiency.

**Step 27: Black Market**  
**Gate:** Gold Reserves, Cultists.  
Acquire reagents. Unlock Soul Battery storage.

**Step 28: Shrine Desecration**  
**Gate:** Wight Leader, Mana.  
Ransack divine site. Harvest potent souls; gain divine essence for mana boost. Recruit heretics.

**Step 29: Flesh Golem**  
**Gate:** Corpse Surplus, Souls, Mana.  
Fuse massive tank minion. Upgrade to regional hideout.

**Step 30: Baron's Fall**  
**Gate:** Army Horde, Curse Mana.  
Seize noble lands. Vast territory expansion; follower surge.

**Step 31: Death Knight**  
**Gate:** Noble Corpse, Souls, Mana, Knowledge.  
Corrupt elite general. Army scales massively.

**Step 32: Academy Plunder**  
**Gate:** Golem Tank, Mana.

**Step 33: Border Skirmishes**  
**Gate:** Large Army, Wave Mana.  
Crush patrols. Secure borderlands; resource overflow.

**Step 34: Acolyte Training**  
**Gate:** Soul Reserves, Followers.  
Empower cultists as summoners. Unlock mass rituals.

**Step 35: Dracolich Rise**  
**Gate:** Ancient Bones (Corpses), Souls, Mana, Tomes.  
Awaken draconic undead. Gain aerial dominance.

**Step 36: Capital Assault**  
**Gate:** Full Horde, Plague Wave Mana.  
Besiege heart of kingdom. Claim capital; endless corpse supply.

**Step 37: Relic Heist**  
**Gate:** Death Knight, Mana.  
Steal anti-divine artifact. Unlock unlimited soul potential.

**Step 38: Lich Transformation**  
**Gate:** Soul Hoard, All Tomes, Massive Mana.  
Bind soul to phylactery. Become immortal lich; power multiplies.

**Step 39: Breach the Heavens**  
**Gate:** Lich Form, Rift Ritual Mana.  
Tear divine gates. Harvest celestial souls.

**Step 40: Gods' Annihilation and Ascension**  
**Gate:** Godslayer Relic, All Resources, Apocalyptic Ritual.  
Shatter pantheon; absorb god-essences. Ascend as eternal Necro-Empress on throne of bones. Vengeance fulfilled.
